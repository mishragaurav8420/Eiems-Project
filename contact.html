Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical quote turnaround time?

We provide initial quotes within 24 hours for most projects. Complex projects requiring detailed engineering analysis may take 2-3 business days.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We support projects from prototype quantities (single units) up to mass production volumes. There is no strict MOQ - we'll work with your requirements.

Do you provide NDA protection?

Yes, we sign NDAs with all clients to protect your intellectual property and confidential information. Your designs and products are fully protected.

Can you help with design for manufacturing (DFM)?

Absolutely! Our engineering team provides comprehensive DFM reviews to optimize your designs for manufacturability, cost reduction, and quality improvement.

What certifications do you hold?

We are certified to IPC-A-610 Class 2 & 3, ESD S20.20, ISO 9001:2015, and follow APQP/PPAP automotive quality standards. Learn more on our Quality page.

Do you offer turnkey solutions including component procurement?

Yes! We offer complete turnkey solutions including global component procurement, PCB fabrication, assembly, testing, and box build services - all from a single source.
