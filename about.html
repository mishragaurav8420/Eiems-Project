Who We Are

Ei EMS India Pvt. Ltd. is a leading electronics manufacturing services (EMS) provider, specializing in end-to-end solutions from concept to production. We combine cutting-edge technology, engineering expertise, and global manufacturing capabilities to deliver world-class products.

With state-of-the-art facilities across India, China, Hong Kong, and the USA, we serve diverse industries including automotive, medical devices, industrial IoT, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for OEMs and technology companies worldwide.
